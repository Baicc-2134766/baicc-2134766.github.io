WildLanterns at Woodland Park Zoo

Through January 30
Explore the zoo like never before with a brand-new lantern festival unlike any in the Northwest! WildLanterns offers a larger-than-life, immersive experience that will wow all ages.

More

Imogen Cunningham: A Retrospective

Through February 6
More than 200 works showcase the innovation and profound influence of a remarkable photographer who pushed the boundaries for both women in the arts and photography as an art form.

More

Disney's Beauty and the Beast

January 21 - February 6
The 5th Avenue Theatre presents an all-new staging of the classic Disney musical.

More

HOCKEY: Faster Than Ever

Through February 27
The world premiere of HOCKEY: Faster Than Ever, a highly-interactive exhibition focusing on the science, history, and culture of hockey, is on view at Pacific Science Center.

More

Cirque du Soleil presents Alegria

January 18 - March 13, 2022
The classic Cirque du Soleil show has been reimagined for a new generation to fall in love with. Under the Big Top in Marymoor Park.

More

Heroes & Villains: The Art of the Disney Costume

Through April 17, 2022
MoPOP and the Walt Disney Archives present more than 70 original pieces that costumed some of Disney's most iconic characters.

More

Frisson: The Richard E. Lang and Jane Lang Davis Collection

Through November 27, 2022
The Lang Collection, a recent gift to SAM from the Friday Foundation, is one of the most significant holdings of Abstract Expressionist paintings and sculpture.
